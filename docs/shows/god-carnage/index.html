God Of Carnage

written by Yasmina Reza
directed by Brandon McCallister
Winter 2018

When two eleven-year-old boys face an altercation on the playground, two sets of Brooklyn parents meet in hopes of clean resolution, but what ensues is nothing short of a total catastrophe. Through her masterful script, Yasmina Reza tests the limits of language, prejudice, and the human breaking point. Experience this captivating and chilling Tony-award winning drama about what it means to fight for your beliefs.

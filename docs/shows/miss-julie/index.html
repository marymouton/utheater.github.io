Miss Julie


In the corseted world of August Strindberg’s Miss Julie, directed by Daniele Wieder, Victorian morality governs the lives and affairs of all. Joined by a chorus, Julie struggles to cast aside the femininity imposed on her and her body. Sex may be the key to freedom, but corset strings are tough to break.

This performance will run approximately 70 minutes with no intermission.

good friday

written by Kristiana Rae Colón
directed by Anna Aguiar Kosicki
Fall 2018

Trapped inside a classroom with a blast of bullets flying just on the other side of the classroom door, this active feminist thriller keeps you on the edge of your seat through its entire duration. Kristiana Rae Colón tackles the escalating topic of gun violence, rape culture, and sexual assault within the modern era of school shootings and viral media.

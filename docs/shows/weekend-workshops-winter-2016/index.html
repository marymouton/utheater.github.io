A Weekend of Workshops (Winter 2016)

Context, written & directed by Kayla Mathisen. The Monkey's Paw, written by W. W. Jacobs, adapted & directed by Brandon McCallister.

Weekend of Workshops offers a stage to directors, devisers and performers to exercise and explore their craft. This Winter, directors probe the depths of the human experience from honest explorations of romantic relationships to raw portrayals of complex family dynamics. Audiences will encounter new work and old, devised and revised. A Weekend of Workshops commits each and every inhabitant of this intimate space to the expansion of the limits of their artistry.

Kayla Mathisen (Director) is a fourth-year in the College majoring in Law, Letters, and Society with a minor in Creative Writing. She wrote and directed Context. She has also directed an abbreviated No Exit, and wrote and directed Comedia Goes West.
